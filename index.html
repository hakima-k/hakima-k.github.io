Hakima Khelifi [GS'17] holds her Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China. She received a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science (2013) from Constantine 2 University, Constantine, Algeria, and a M.Sc. degree (2015) from Mohamed El Bachir El Ibrahimi University, Bordj Bou-Arreridj, Algeria.

Ms. Khelifi received the Best Paper Award of the IEEE GLOBECOM in 2018, and the Excellent Student Award at the Beijing Institute of Technology in 2017-18 and 2018-19, as well as she received the Chinese Government Outstanding International Student Scholarship in the year of 2019

Moreover, Ms. Khelifi is serving as a Technical Program Committee Member or Reviewer in different International Conferences and Workshops. Her current research interest includes Next-Generation Networking and Internet, Vehicular Ad Hoc Networks, Internet of Things, Information-Centric Networking, and Named Data Networking. She is a Graduate Student Member of IEEE.
