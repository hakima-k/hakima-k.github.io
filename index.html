About

Hakima Khelifi is currently working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer science, University of Bordj Bou Arreridj, Algeria. Dr. Khelifi holds a Doctor of Philosophy degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China. She received a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science (2013) from Constantine 2 University, Constantine, Algeria, and an M.Sc. degree (2015) from Mohamed El Bachir El Ibrahimi University, Bordj Bou-Arreridj, Algeria.

Dr. Khelifi received the Best Paper Award of the IEEE GLOBECOM in 2018, and the Excellent Student Award at the Beijing Institute of Technology in 2017-18 and 2018-19, as well as the Chinese Government Outstanding International Student Scholarship in 2019. Her Ph.D. dissertation received the Excellent Thesis Award in 2020.

Moreover, Dr. Khelifi is serving as a Technical Program Committee Member or Reviewer in different International Conferences and Workshops. Her current research interest includes Next-Generation Networking and Internet, Vehicular Ad Hoc Networks, Internet of Things, Information-Centric Networking, Named Data Networking, Mobile Edge Computing, and Big Data Analytics.
